OUR LOCATIONS

NUWARA ELIYA (MAIN)

Nuwara Eliya, known for its cool climate and scenic beauty, is home to our main hospital. With modern facilities and personalized care, it offers a unique healing environment. The tranquil surroundings of this picturesque city create the perfect setting for patient recovery and wellness with the mindset of peace and speedy recovery for anyone just by the nature around you.

NEGOMBO BRANCH

Situated on the western coast of Sri Lanka, Negombo is a bustling coastal city known for its sandy beaches, vibrant fishing industry, and historical landmarks. The city is easily accessible, located just a short drive from Bandaranaike International Airport, making it a convenient hub for both locals and international visitors. With a mix of modern infrastructure and a rich cultural heritage, Negombo is an ideal location for healthcare services.
